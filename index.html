Ulam spiral

An Ulam spiral is a way to order the natural numbers numbers which visually demonstrates areas with a high density of primes. Prime numbers are demarcated by red cells in the grid below.

Good question! Whilst some areas of high prime density is expected, the extent of the patterns you are seeing with your very own eyes in the image below represent regularity that no current mathematical formula has yet fully captured.
